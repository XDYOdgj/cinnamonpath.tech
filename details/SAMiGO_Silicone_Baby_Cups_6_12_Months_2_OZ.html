SAMiGO Silicone Baby Cups for Independent Drinking

Designed with your baby's development in mind, these silicone baby cups help little ones take their first steps toward drinking from an open cup with confidence and ease.

Why Choose SAMiGO?

The cups feature an ergonomic, compact design that fits comfortably in your baby's tiny hands, making it easier for them to grip and drink. The weighted base ensures stability and prevents accidental tipping, giving parents peace of mind during feeding sessions.

With a 2 oz capacity, these cups are perfectly sized to support the right amount of liquid for every sip, encouraging healthy drinking habits without overwhelming your baby. The soft, rounded edges are gentle on sensitive gums and ideal for chewing, making them versatile for baby led weaning or hydration.

  • Perfectly shaped for easy handling by little hands
