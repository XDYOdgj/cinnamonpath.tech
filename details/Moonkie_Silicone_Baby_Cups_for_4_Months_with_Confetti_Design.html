Moonkie Silicone Baby Cups

Designed for 4 months and up, these cups help transition your baby from breast or bottle feeding to drinking independently. With a soft, safe silicone material and a playful confetti design, they make mealtime a fun and engaging experience for your little one and a stress-free one for you.

Why Parents Love These Cups

These Moonkie Silicone Baby Cups are the perfect tool for baby-led weaning, encouraging your baby to develop fine motor skills and oral coordination. The ergonomic shape fits tiny hands, allowing for easy gripping and a better grasp of the cup during feeding.

The 2oz capacity is ideal for helping your baby learn to sip without feeling overwhelmed, promoting a smooth transition to drinking from a cup. Plus, the inner portion lines ensure controlled sips and help with portion management, making mealtime more organized and less messy.

Safe & Durable Design

Manufactured from BPA-free platinum silicone, these cups are gentle on your baby's gums and teeth, and safe for use during teething. The thick body and weighted base provide stability, reducing spills and keeping your baby comfortable during feeding times.

Lightweight yet sturdy, they're easy to clean and perfect for use at home or on the go. Whether you're preparing for a baby shower or looking for a gift for new parents, the confetti design adds a touch of playfulness and charm to every feeding session.
